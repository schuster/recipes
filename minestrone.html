Minestrone

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 ½ cups tomato sauce (about 1 jar)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups frozen spinach
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup pasta
  • 1 cup cannelini benas or chickpeas, with juices
  • grated Parmesan cheese
  1. In a large pot over medium heat, heat the olive oil. Add onion, basil, and oregano and saute about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, tomato sauce, salt, and pepper adn bring to a low simmer.
  3. Add spinach, vegetables, and pasta, and simmer about 10 minutes or utnil pasta is tender.
  4. Add beans, lower the heat, and cook for 10 minutes. Serve garnished with Parmesan cheese.
