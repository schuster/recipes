Chicken Squares

Yields 4 squares

Source: Grandma (Gloria) Schuster

Ingredients:

  • 3 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3 tablespoons margarine, melted
  • 2 cups cooked, cubed chicken
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon chopped pimiento, if desired
  • 8 oz Pillsbury can of crescent rolls
  • 3/4 cup seasoned croutons, crushed
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°.
  2. In medium bowl, blend cream cheese and 2 tablespoons margarine (reserve 1 tablespoon) until smooth.
  3. Add the next six (6) ingredients; mix well.
  4. Separate crescent dough into 4 rectangles; firmly press perforations to seal.
  5. Spoon 1/2 cup meat mixture onto center of each rectangle. Pull 4 corners of dough to top center of chicken mixture, twist slightly and seal edges. Brush tops with reserved 1 tablespoon margarine; dip in crouton crumbs.
  6. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown.

Note: Mom usually didn't put in as much margarine as called for here
