Manicotti

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of manicotti (about 14 tubes)
  • ¾ pounds ground beef
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 4 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 large jar of tomato sauce, around 45 ounces
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • ½ teaspoon oregano
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the manicotti and boil until they are just slightly flexible, about 3–4 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. While the pot of water is coming to a boil, heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and ground beef and brown the beef. Drain the beef and empty it into a bowl.
  4. Mix in the cottage cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, mozzarella, and salt into the bowl with the browned meat.
  5. Stuff the manicotti with the meat-and-cheese mixture, arranging in a single layer in a greased 9x13 casserole dish.
  6. Add any leftover filling to the top of the manicotti, then cover with tomato sauce. Sprinkle the Parmesan and oregano on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for another 15 minutes.
