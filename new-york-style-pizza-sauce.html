New York-Style Pizza Sauce

Makes about ¾ cup, enough for one large pie

Source: The United States of Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • ½ cup canned Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon grated Pecorino Romano

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic cloves and cook until they are golden brown, about 2 minutes. Remove from the heat and let cool.
  2. Transfer the garlic and the oil from the pan to a food processor fitted with the metal blade. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper and puree until smooth.
  3. Add the grated Romano and pulse to combine.
  4. Let the sauce sit for 1 hour, either on the counter or in the refrigerator, to allow the flavors to meld.
