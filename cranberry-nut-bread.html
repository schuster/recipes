Cranberry Nut Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all purpose or unbleached flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ cup shortening or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon grated orange peel
  • ¾ cup orange juice
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries, coarsely chopped
  • ½ cup chopped nuts
  1. Heat oven to 350 °F. Grease (not oil) bottom only of 9x5 or 8x4-inch loaf pan.
  2. In large bowl, combine first 5 ingredients.
  3. Using pastry blender, cut in shortening until particles are size of coarse meal.
  4. Stir in peel, juice, and egg just until dry ingredients are moistened.
  5. Fold in cranberries and nuts.
  6. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 50 to 60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  8. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan. Cool completely.

TIPS: A well-greased (not oiled) 12-cup fluted tube pan may be substituted for the loaf pan. Bake at 350°F for 30 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
