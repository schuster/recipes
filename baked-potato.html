Baked Potato

Serves 1

Ingredients:

  • 1 large Russet potato
  • canola oil
  • Salt
  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Wash and scrub the potato under cold running water. Poke holes all over the potato with a fork, place in a bowl, and lightly coat with oil and salt.
  2. Place potato on center rack of oven and let bake for 1 hour, or until skin is crisp but flesh is soft.
