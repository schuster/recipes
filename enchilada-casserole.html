Enchilada Casserole

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 1-2 cups grated cheese (Colby or Monterey Jack)
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  1. Brown the meat and onion, and drain off the fat. Add soup and enchilada sauce and stir.
  2. In a greased casserole dish, layer tortillas, meat mixture, and cheese. Repeat twice more.
  3. Bake at 350° for 35-40 minutes, or until heated through.
