Spinach Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 package spinach
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ½ pound cooked bacon, diced
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Fresh mushrooms
  • ½ cup oil
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • salt, to taste
  1. In a large salad bowl, combine spinach, croutons, bacon, eggs, onion, and mushrooms. Add the remaining ingredients to a jar and shake or blend until smooth. Pour the dressing over salad just prior to serving and toss well.
