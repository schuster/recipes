Garlic Pretzels

Ingredients:

  • 2–4 bags of pretzels
  • 1 small bottle of popcorn oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp dill weed
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix powder
  1. Mix everything together, using about ¾ of the bottle of oil, until oil is absorbed and pretzels are coated.
  2. Put in brown paper bag and shake gently to remove excess oil.
  3. Lay out pretzels on brown paper bags for several hours to absorb the oil.
