Gnocchi with roasted mushrooms, peas, chives, and garlic cream

Serves 4

Source: Kenji's Instagram and Serious Eats: Easy Roasted Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb mushrooms, rinsed, trimmed, and quartered
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Thyme sprigs
  • 1 clove of garlic, smashed and finely chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 lb gnocchi
  • Frozen peas
  • Chives, chopped
  1. Toss the mushrooms with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread the mushrooms on a rimmed baking sheet lined with foil and top with thyme sprigs. Roast in a 375 °F oven for 15 minutes. Carefully drain off the liquid, then continue roasting until the mushrooms are browned and chewy but not crisp, about another 30 minutes.
  2. Gently reduce the cream with garlic until moderately thickened.
  3. While the cream is reducing, boil a pot of water and cook the gnocchi. Drain the water, reserving a small amount of pasta water.
  4. Toss pasta, mushrooms, peas, and sauce with a splash of pasta water over high heat. Top with chives and serve.
