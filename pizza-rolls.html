Pizza Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 1 can Pillsbury pizza dough
  • 8 oz grated mozzarella or Parmesan cheese
  • 1 can pizza sauce
  • pizza toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers)
  1. Preheat oven to 375°. Open pizza dough and spread out on a baking tray. Spread mozzarella and pizza toppings on the dough. Roll up the dough lengthwise so that you end up with a roll a little over a foot long.
  2. Put the tray in the oven and bake for about 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  3. When the roll is almost done, heat the sauce and put it in a bowl for serving.
  4. Take out the roll and slice into 1-inch-wide pieces. Serve with the dipping sauce.
