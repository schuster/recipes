Mom's Chili (in the slow cooker)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey, browned
  • 2 cans kidney beans, drained
  • 1 green pepper, chopped and seeded
  • 1-2 small-to-medium onions, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large and 1 small can diced tomatoes
  • 2-3 tablespoons chili powder
  • up to 1 teaspoon black pepper
    • n
  1. Mix all ingredients in slow cooker. Cook on low for about 8 hours.
