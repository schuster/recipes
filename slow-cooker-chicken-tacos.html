Slow Cooker chicken tacos

Ingredients:

  • boneless, skinless chicken parts
  • 1 jar salsa
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • tortillas and/or taco shells
  • taco fixings
  1. Put chicken, salsa, and seasoning into slow cooker and cook for 6 hours on low.
  2. Remove chicken, pull apart with forks, and serve.
