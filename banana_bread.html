Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 ripe bananas
  • ¾-1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • pinch of salt
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 ½ cup all-purpose flour
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°.
  2. With a wooden spon, mix butter and bananas in a large mixing bown. Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over mixture and mix in. Mix in flour.
  3. Pour mixture into buttered 4x8 loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour, or until done.
