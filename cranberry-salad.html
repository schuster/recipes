Cranberry Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 small packages red gelatin (raspberry)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 can cranberry sauce
  • 1 20oz can crushed pineapple
  • 3 cups (12 oz) fresh cranberries
  • 1 whole orange, diced medium (with the peel)
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, diced medium (with the peel)

Instructions

  1. Mix the gelatin with 2 cups boiling water in a large bowl and set aside.
  2. Grind all remaining ingredients together in a blender (cut them into small pieces as needed ahead of time). Mix into the bowl with the gelatin.
