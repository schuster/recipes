Pears and Craisin Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 ripe pear, diced
  • 1 Granny Smith apple, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup slivered almonds, toasted
  • 1/2 cup craisins
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese, shredded
  • poppy seed dressing (to taste)
  • 4 oz blue cheese

Combine all ingredients except for blue cheese. Toss lightly with dressing. Serve with blue cheese on the side
