Potato Soup

Source: Zoe Wagner

Ingredients:

  • 32 oz chicken broth
  • 3.5 lbs Russet potatoes (about 4 large potatoes), peeled and diced into bite-sized pieces
  • ½ large onion, chopped fine
  • 3 stalks celery, chopped
  • 3 large carrots, shredded
  • 2 Tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 Tbsp dried dill weed
  • 6 strips bacon, chopped into small pieces
  • ⅓-½ box instant mashed potatoes mix
  • 3 cups heavy whipping cream
  • salt
  • black pepper
  1. Bring 1 quart of water plus the chicken broth to a boil in a large stock pot. Add vegetables, herbs, and bacon and let boil for one hour, or until vegetables are soft.
  2. Gradually add mashed potato mix and stir until soup has a loose oatmeal consistency.
  3. Slowly add whipping cream. Heat through but do not boil. Add salt and pepper to taste.
