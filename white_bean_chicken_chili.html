White Bean Chicken Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 can Great northern beans
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 4.5 oz green chiles
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups (1 can) chicken broth
  • 2 Tbsp corn starch for thickening (optional)
  • Monterey Jack cheese for garnish
  1. Heat oil and onion in a large pot.
  2. Add chicken and cook until chicken is cooked through.
  3. Rinse and drain the beans and chiles. Add beans, chiles, garlic, cumin, pepper, cayenne pepper, and broth and bring to a boil.
  4. To thicken (optional), mix corn starch with 2 Tbsp water and shake in tightly-sealed container. Slowly add mixture to pot until soup has reached desired thickness.
  5. Serve with Monterey Jack cheese on top.
