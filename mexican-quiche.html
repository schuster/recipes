Mexican Quiche

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 4oz can minced green chilies
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 lb shredded Jack cheese
  1. Melt butter in 9 inch square pan. Beat eggs lightly in large bowl. Blend in flour and baking powder. Add melted butter, cheese, and Jack cheese and mix until just blended.
  2. Pour mixture into pan and bake at 400 degrees for 15 mins. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue baking an additional 35 to 40 mins. Cut into squares and serve hot. Recipe can be doubled and baked in a 9x13 baking pan.
